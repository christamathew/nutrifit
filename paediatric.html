Symptoms

Why and when should I consult a Pediatrician?

Paediatricians manage the health of your child, including physical, mental health and behavioural issues. They are trained to diagnose and treat childhood illnesses, from minor health problems to serious diseases. Your child needs to see a paediatrician if they are showing any of the below symptoms

  • Persistent Cough for more than a Week.
  • Chest pain
  • Abdominal pain or Colic
  • High Fever of 101F not subsiding with medication
  • Headache
  • Cold, severe sneezing, sore throat and runny nose.
