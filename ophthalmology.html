Symptoms

Why and when should I consult an Ophthalmologist?

An Ophthalmologist specializes in eye and vision care. They diagnose and treat all eye diseases, perform eye surgery, and prescribe, and fit eyeglasses and contact lenses to correct vision problems. Some of the signs and symptoms for which an eye specialist online should be consulted are:

  • Migraine: Ocular migraine causes aura or eye disturbances. Medications are usually prescribed for chronic pain and to reduce symptoms.
  • Headache: If you are unable to manage your headaches the specialist will perform an eye exam and find the cause for it and provide a detailed treatment plan for the same.
  • Shingles: Sometimes shingles rash appears around or inside the eye. and need to be treated with antiviral medications.
  • Blurred vision: Sudden changes in vision or blurred visions is a symptom that should be checked if you have halos or blurred or dimmed vision.
  • Itchy Eyes: Eyes can get irritated and itchy due to allergies or foreign objects. See the doctor when you have excess goop in the eye, eyes turn red, have a sty of the eye, have light sensitivity.
  • Grittiness: It is a type of eye irritation where you have a feeling of sand in the eye.
  • Photophobia: Though it is common as you age if you develop this condition rapidly consult your doctor as it may be a sign of some other eye disorder.
