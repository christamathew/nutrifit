Why and when should I consult an ENT Specialist?

ENT doctors specialize in the treatment of ear, nose, and throat and related areas in your head and neck. They treat ear infection, hearing loss, trouble with balance, allergies, sinusitis, tonsillitis, swallowing, voice issues along with with snoring or apnea. \nThere are many signs and symptoms that act as indicators for you to meet an ENT specialist and through an online doctor consultation. Some of the symptoms that warrant consulting an ENT specialist are:

  • Runny Nose: It is one of the common issues that affect even a healthy human at some point or another. The causes for a runny nose are many and most common is colds and allergies. But If you have a fever and a cough along with a postnasal drip you should see a doctor. A nose doctor online will prescribe antihistamines and other medications to treat it.
  • Hearing Loss: Problems with hearing or temporary hearing loss can happen when you hear loud noise or music. But if it persists, consult an ear specialist to ensure there is no permanent hearing loss.
  • Nose Irritation: An allergy doctor can treat nose irritation as it happens usually due to allergies or a common cold. But if it is persistent then an ENT specialist can treat problems that affect the sinus and affect the breathing and smell.
  • Sore throat: It is common in the cold season and hence most people use home remedies to treat it. But if you have a sore throat where even drinking a sip of water is difficult then it is recommended that you book an appointment to rule out serious issues.
  • Throat infection: It can affect speech, swallowing, and other activities and an ENT doctor can manage and treat the problems that caused the infection.
